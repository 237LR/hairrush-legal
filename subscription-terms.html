Subscription Services

HairRush offers subscription services that provide access to premium features within the app.

Subscription Plans

  • Monthly Subscription: $4.99 per month

Free Trial

  • New users receive a 2-day free trial of premium features
  • No charges will be made during the trial period
  • After the trial period ends, your selected subscription will automatically begin unless cancelled

Billing and Renewal

  • Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase
  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
  • Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period
  • You can manage and cancel your subscriptions by going to your account settings on the App Store

Refund Policy

  • Refunds are handled by Apple according to their App Store policies
  • We cannot directly process refunds for in-app purchases
  • To request a refund, please contact Apple Support or visit reportaproblem.apple.com

Price Changes

  • We reserve the right to change subscription prices
  • Any price changes will be communicated to you in advance
  • Price changes will not affect current subscription periods but will apply to renewals
